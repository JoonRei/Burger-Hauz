The Burger Hauz Story

Our commitment to quality, community, and the perfect burger.

Our Background

Founded in 2025 as a passion project from a small food truck, Burger Hauz quickly grew into a favorite, known for its commitment to fresh, local ingredients.

Our Mission

To handcraft the most delicious, high-quality burgers and provide a welcoming experience that brings friends and families together.

Our Vision

To be the leading gourmet burger destination, continuously innovating our menu while expanding our reach to new communities responsibly and sustainably.
